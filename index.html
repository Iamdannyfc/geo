Our Focus

  • Roads: The state has a limited network of paved roads, with many rural areas relying on unpaved roads that are often impassable and unmotorable.
  • Railways: Niger State has no operational railway lines.
  • Bridges: There are relatively few number of bridges and no flyovers , making transportation within the state less flexible.

    Although the current government embarked on "URBAN RENEWAL PROJECTS" to build state of the art roads and flyovers accross Niger state.
